INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU ON THIS WEBSITE AND HOW WE COLLECT IT

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Website, including information:

  • By which you and any co-buyers may be personally identified, such as first name, last name, title, home telephone number, cell phone number, e-mail address, street address, city, zip code, lot number, metropolitan location, state, date of birth, social security number, Online ID, industry, preferred method of contact, and emergency contact (“personal information”).

  • Total annual household income, whether you rent or own, whether you are working with a realtor, as well as their name, company, and e-mail, loan type preference, whether you are pre-qualified for a loan (if so, the prequalification letter, the name of the loan officer and his or her e-mail, the lender name, and whether you are rolling in closing costs), whether you are paying in cash (if so, an asset statement less than 30 days old), whether you are relocating, whether you chatted with an online representative, whether your current home listed with a realtor, and preferred appointment date and time.

  • As to realtors, first name, last name, office telephone number, e-mail address, street address, city, zip code, metropolitan location, and community.

  • Any additional information you provide to us.

  • About your internet connection, including the equipment you use to access our Website, usage details, and geolocation.

We collect this information:

  • Directly from you when you provide it to us.

  • Automatically as you navigate through the site. Information collected automatically may include usage details, IP address, and information collected through cookies and other tracking technologies.

  • From third parties, for example, our website management vendor and lead generators.
